PROJECT CHARTER
Project: Develop and Implement a Department-Wide Community Engagement Framework
Report Date: 2026-06-22

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This project aims to establish a comprehensive community engagement framework for the Philadelphia Police Department. It will enhance communication and collaboration between the police and the community, fostering trust and transparency. The initiative will involve the development of standard operating procedures and ongoing support for district-level engagement efforts.

STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE
Advance Trust with the Community and Partners

PROJECT PURPOSE
The purpose of this project is to create a structured approach to community engagement that aligns with the Department's goals of building trust and legitimacy. It will ensure consistent and effective communication and engagement practices across all districts.

KEY DELIVERABLES
- Community engagement framework document
- Standard operating procedures for community policing
- Training materials for district-level personnel
- Implementation plan for ongoing support and evaluation

HIGH LEVEL REQUIREMENTS
- Involvement of community stakeholders in the development process
- Alignment with existing departmental policies and practices
- Training for officers on community engagement techniques
- Regular feedback mechanisms from community members

PROJECT CONSTRAINTS
- Limited budget for training and resources
- Potential resistance to change within the department
- Need for alignment with other ongoing initiatives
- Time constraints for implementation within the strategic plan timeline

PROJECT ASSUMPTIONS
- Community stakeholders will actively participate in the process
- Department personnel will be receptive to new engagement strategies
- Sufficient resources will be allocated for training and implementation
- The framework will be adaptable to changing community needs

SCHEDULE MILESTONES
- Framework development completion by September 2026
- Training sessions for district personnel by November 2026
- Initial implementation of the framework by January 2027
- Evaluation and feedback collection by March 2027

SUCCESS CRITERIA
- Increased community participation in engagement activities
- Positive feedback from community surveys regarding police transparency
- Improved perceptions of police legitimacy in community feedback
- Successful implementation of standard operating procedures across all districts

HIGH LEVEL RISKS
- Lack of community engagement leading to ineffective implementation
- Insufficient training resulting in inconsistent application of the framework
- Potential backlash from community members if expectations are not met
- Changes in leadership affecting project continuity

BUDGET
To be defined.
